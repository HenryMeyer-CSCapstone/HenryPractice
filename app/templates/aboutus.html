About Us

Background

My initial research topic was how blinking affects the health of people. I found that blinking is a very necessary function of the human body and it is good to blink often, at least 15 times a minute.

Danger of not blinking

Not blinking is very dangerous, as you can get things in your eyes easier, develop dry eye, headaches, or an eye infection

About Me

I am Henry Meyer, a senior in the Oakland Tech Computer Science Academy and I am also a rower. I made this website because as a student athlete, it is important to blink a lot and I want to inform others on the effects of not blinking. Being sure to blink often has positively influenced my life by a great margin and I would like to share that same change with others!
